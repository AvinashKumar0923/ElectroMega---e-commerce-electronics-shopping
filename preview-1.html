Overview

Unique Design and Durable Build
Flaunting a back glass infused with colour, the iPhone 15 is sure to grab eyeballs everywhere you go. With a custom dual-ion exchange process and aerospace-grade aluminium enclosure, this phone is robust. Plus, its Ceramic Shield front provides protection against scratches, maintaining the pristine look of your device.
Vivid Display
Experience the brilliance of the 6.1-inch 2556x1179 Super Retina XDR display, which is bright enough to be visible in direct sunlight. Whether you're outdoors on a sunny day or by the poolside, you'll appreciate the enhanced visibility and clarity this display provides.
Dynamic Island
Stay updated with Dynamic Island, which conveniently presents alerts and Live Activities without interrupting your tasks. Whether you're tracking your next ride, checking the flight status, or answering calls, you won't miss important updates while multitasking.
Advanced Camera System
Capture stunning photos with this iPhone's advanced camera system, which features a 48MP main camera, a 12MP ultra-wide camera, and a 12MP 2x telephoto camera. The 12MP TrueDepth front camera delivers high-quality selfies and clear video calls. Moreover, its smart HDR technology ensures optimal lighting for both subjects and backgrounds, resulting in vivid colours and lifelike skin tones. And Night mode activates automatically in low-light environments, producing vibrant hues and sharp details.
