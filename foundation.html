Vision
At ElectroMega, there has always been great intent and enthusiasm to support initiatives on the ground. Be it supporting grassroots-level interventions, celebrating festivals with the underprivileged to spread cheer, or commemorating days of relevance as a mark of commitment to the causes
Overview
At ElectroMega, we align our Corporate Social Responsibility activities to the overarching theme of "Building India Together". With this goal, 'ElectroMega Cares' was launched to serve, support, and empower unskilled and underprivileged sections of society. To institutionalize these efforts of Flipkart in creating sustainable social impact and to streamline our CSR program delivery, we went on to establish the "Flipkart Foundation" in 2022.
Mission
The Foundation focuses on continuing the work done through ElectroMega Cares, in various sectors to ensure maximum reach and sustainable impact.
