━━━━   Factors to consider while buying a Mobile Phone   ━━━━

If you want to buy a smartphone, the first thing to decide is the operating system you may be comfortable with -- Android or iOS. Designed only for iPhone, iOS is known for its speed and security. On the contrary, Android mobiles are more common since they run on an open-source OS and are widely available. Several brands such as Samsung, Oppo, and Xiaomi, among others, offer Android phones. Buy the latest Android smartphone online at easy EMI When it comes to network, 5G network is finally here. Almost every smartphone even the budgeted ones are equipped with 5G. Buy a new range of 4G Mobiles Phones Online. Cameras are getting better with each passing month and mobile phone manufacturers are racing to build phones that are sleeker, faster, smarter, and better. Camera with more than just megapixels. Good quality photos are a function of factors like ISO levels, aperture as well as the speed of autofocus. Some other important features to consider while choosing a mobile phone are its display size, RAM, storage capacity, and battery life. A phone with a 5.5 – 6-inch HD or QHD display is usually an ideal option for most of us. RAM is another crucial factor as it stores Operating System, apps, and data that are currently in use. Where up to 4GB RAM phones are good for calling, basic web browsing, basic social media usage, 8GB RAM mobile phones are ideal for heavy video watching, gaming, and e-reading. While Smartphones are the most popular phones today, some still like the old-school charm of a Feature Phone . With physical buttons and limited features, these mobile phones are the first choice of people who want a no-fuss phone merely to stay in touch.
